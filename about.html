About InterviewClear

Built by developers, for the next generation of developers.

Our Mission

InterviewClear exists to remove one specific barrier to a first tech job: scattered, inconsistent, or paywalled interview preparation content. We bring company-wise patterns, programming fundamentals, HR guidance, and aptitude practice into one free, organized place.

Who We're For

Engineering students preparing for campus placements, CSE graduates applying to service-based IT companies, and career-switchers building the fundamentals needed for a first developer role.

What Makes Us Different

  • Every question includes a clear, original explanation — not copy-pasted forum answers.
  • Company pages reflect real, current selection processes rather than generic templates.
  • Every major category is downloadable as a formatted PDF for offline revision.
  • No login walls, no paywalls — the core content is free.

Content Accuracy

Interview processes change frequently. While we work to keep company-specific information current, always cross-check the latest process directly with the company's official careers page before an interview.
